## Slide 1 Notes (0:00-0:20)

"I used to miss meaningful deadlines even when I was working hard. The issue was not ambition. The issue was that my commitments were private and drift stayed invisible until it was too late."

"DueForge exists to fix that execution gap."

## Slide 2 Notes (0:20-0:40)

"The purpose of this company is simple: turn intent into evidence-backed execution."

"In DueForge, commitments are visible, proof is expected, and drift gets surfaced early."

## Slide 3 Notes (0:40-1:00)

"Our core user is the execution-strained builder: founders and operators with high intent and inconsistent follow-through."

"The pain is expensive: missed deadlines, lost trust, and broken momentum."

## Slide 4 Notes (1:00-1:20)
